PROJECT MOTIVATION

We were motivated and empowered to create Slim &Fit website after coming to terms with the bleak reality of COVID-19 and how it has seized away the freedom from our youth and the elderly. Therefore, we wanted to create a health and fitness related website which would allow the users to carry out their usual routine despite not being able to physically visit their local gyms and other health related facilities. This website would be the most perfect website for all the health-conscious people who want to still pursue with their fitness and health related goals despite being struck by this pandemic. We assure to provide the most incredible health services to allow individuals to stay fit and healthy.

Not many people are self-driven to look after themselves, especially when they are surrounded by such catastrophe. So, our primary goal is to instigate enthusiasm and motivation amongst people to stay healthy regardless of the situation. By creating the website, we will definitely be going above and beyond our current IT knowledge and skills as we will learn and explore new technologies in each and every stage of the development starting from the design stage all the way up to the evaluation stage. We as a group will enrich our IT skills in different areas such as designing, programming, communication and writing skills.

We did not use our IT idea from last semester, but we were inspired from our project that we did in UCD. As a group we merged the two themes together, which included fitness and health and thus came up with the idea to create Fit & Slim website. This is the most perfect website which instils a level of motivation amongst people to stay healthy. Although our UCD project was not that advanced or complexed but we gathered the inspiration from that project and used that as a foundation to build upon the project. Furthermore, we have added some incredible and advanced features such as goal setter, workout tracker, memberships , services and fitness products to appeal to the user’s interest, consequently allowing the users to stay motivated in the face of the outbreak of deadly virus.

Aims and Goals

“Turn Fat into FIT”
This is the main aim of the Fit&Slim project. In this current pandemic situation we want people to stay healthy and physically fit by just sitting at home. The aim of this project is to help people on daily basis to set their goals and also track them. Providing personalised services can manifold the effects. Moreover, providing healthy products at their doorsteps can be more beneficial.

The first aim is to make sure that the user engages with some sort of physical exercise on daily basis. To do this, our website will provide goal setter and goal tracker. Goal setter will help the user to set daily goals on regular basis on the other hand tracker will help them to make sure whether the goal is achieved or not. This feature will help the user to get motivated and help them to participate in some sort of activities which will provide them healthy mind and body.

Other aim is to provide personal services at home. Through this services page users can access to workout videos, daily plans, diet charts and recipes. Moreover, this service also includes personal training sessions with trainers and dieticians. Most of the time people need one on one guidance to maintain their health and share their problems. So, this feature will help the user to get professional guidance to maintain their wellbeing.

Additionally, providing fitness products is one of the key feature. As we all know that most of the people like snacks and stuff during their leisure time. So our website will provide healthy snacks which will help the user to maintain their health and enjoy the munching of snacks at same time. Our website will provide eatables as well as other equipments like yoga mats, skipping ropes, exercising tools and many more. This all will help user to get everything under one roof in order to maintain their health. They need not to go to other places or sites to get all the required things. Everything will be provided to the user at one click. The main aim of our website is to keep the user as motivated as we can to help them be fit and fine.

Our fourth main feature is the workout tracker. This is a super cool feature which allows the users to understand how well they are doing in terms of their fitness by filling in the required fields and getting a personalized summary. Just to name a few features, users will be asked to enter their calorie intake, amount of sleep and exercise to see a customized generated report. However, if the user wants to add more features, they wont be able to unless they are logged in. if they are new to the website, they would be re-directed to the registration page to register in order to access more features and servcies.
