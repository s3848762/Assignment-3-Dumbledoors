Running Our Project


MVF 1: Login Page

By clicking on the user icon in the top right corner of the nav bar, you are directed to login page. As a first-time user you will was not to register, select the create a new Account button to be directed to a registration page. Fill in the required fields and register your account. Then continue to login and log in with your user details.


MVF 2: Products Page

Products page and search function. By going to the products, you can select an item of your choice and be directed to that products page. You will be able to fill in quantity, see the price and add it to your cart. You can search for a specific item in the search bar, try typing in soup to bring that item up.


MVF 3: Services Page

The Services feature we have allowed the users to see what services, by clicking on a service takes you to that specific page with a brief description. Click on get this service to be directed to the login page, sign in to get access to the service.


MVF 4: Workout Tracker

Workout Tracker is accessible from the services page. Add any of the specific workout tracker functions by clicking “Add walk” place a time and it will show underneath. All exercises are kept tracked. You can get a summary and feed back from the system, by clicking the “get Summary” and depending on the activity the system will calculate and produce relevant feedback.


MVF 5: Goal Setter and Feedback

This page is split int two, the first is the “Set Your Goal” and the other one is the “Feedback” section. The first section allows the users to name their goal and describe what it is as well as when they would like to achieve that goal. Once the user clicks on submit, a summary of what they have entered would show up which will act as a reminder of what needs to be achieved. The second section of the same page presents the users with the opportunity to provide themselves with some feedback as to what extent they were able to achieve their goal. This allows the users to reflect upon what they have achieved and to what extent.


EVF: Checkout Page

This is accessible after adding a product to the cart. The page will appear as a Billing address form. It will require you to fill out all details for shipping and payment information. Once filled confirm order. A pop up prompt will appear if it was successful.
